The Best Books According to Sarah

Some of my favorite books of all time (in no particular order)

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

A captivating story about fame, love, and the price of success. Taylor Jenkins Reid at her finest.

The Midnight Library

A beautiful exploration of life's infinite possibilities and the choices that shape us.

The Book Thief

Narrated by Death himself, this WWII story is both heartbreaking and life-affirming.

Project Hail Mary

Andy Weir's space adventure that's both scientifically fascinating and emotionally engaging.

The House in the Cerulean Sea

A cozy, heartwarming fantasy that feels like a warm hug in book form.
